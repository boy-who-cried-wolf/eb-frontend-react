Frequently Asked Questions

Does Evolve Broker replace my aggregator CRM?

No. Your aggregator CRM stays; Evolve Broker sits alongside it as the automation and workflow layer so you do less double‑handling,
not more.

How long does implementation take?

For a typical small team, expect a few weeks from kickoff to live use, because we’re configuring a proven blueprint rather than building
from scratch.

What if I’m not “techy”?

That’s okay. Evolve Broker is built by brokers for brokers – you follow a clear workflow while we handle the complexity in the background.

What size brokerage is Evolve Broker best for?

Right now we’re focused on independent brokers and lean teams of roughly 1–9 brokers who are already busy and want to scale without a big back office.

Do I need a banking or credit background to get value?

Not strictly, but many of our best‑fit brokers have banking/credit or 3–5+ years’ experience and already know how they want to serve clients.

What if I’m not selected for the first group?

We’ll let you know, keep you on our priority list, and update you as we open up the next wave of places.

How is pricing structured?

Pricing is premium and reflects the depth of the solution; for the first group we’re open to more flexible arrangements in exchange for your feedback.

Will this help with back‑book retention and clawback risk?

Yes. Evolve Broker monitors your back book and surfaces repricing/refinance opportunities so you can act before clients drift away.

I already use a few tools. Why would I change?

Because Evolve Broker pulls those jobs into one coherent workflow, reducing log‑ins, copying, and manual glue between systems.

Is this about “earning more by doing less”?

No. It’s about doing better work with clients in less time; more revenue follows from better systems and better client outcomes, not shortcuts.
